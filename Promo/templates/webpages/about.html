About Project

About Project

As more users joined social media platforms like Facebook, Youtube, Instagram, Pinterest, Snapchat, and Twitter, many of them became content creators themselves, curating and crafting their own stories, photos, and videos for others to see.This is how social media influencers were born, and quickly attracted the attention of marketers. Due to heavy traffic of common people following and loving the content of these youtube influencers they tend to watch them and kind of follow them. So if any brand want to expand their business an sell the product to the common people these youtube influencers would be a great way to do their paid collaborations / brand endorsement.
